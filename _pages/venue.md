Workshop will take place in the main building of ETH (“Hauptgebäude” – HG) (tentatively) in [room D3.2](https://ethz.ch/staffnet/en/service/rooms-and-buildings/rectorate-rooms/pictures/hg.html#par_slideshow_12).
